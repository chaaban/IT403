Rock, Paper, Scissor game.
press 1 to choose "rock".
press 2 to choose "paper".
press 3 to choose "scissor".
The game ends when the user press 0.
every round the computer choose by random rock, paper, or scissor
at the end of each round there is a scoreboard that indicated who is wining
at the end of the game the winner will be displayed on the screen based on
their score.
